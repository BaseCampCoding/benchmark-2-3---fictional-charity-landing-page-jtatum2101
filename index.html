What is The Helping Hand Foundation?

For many years, the problems with drug abuse and addictions have been a major problems in America and other countries. These drugs vary from prescription drugs to hardcore drugs like heroin and meth. The helping hand was founded by Jeremiah Tatum in late 2020. The Helping Hand Foundation is for families that have broken apart due to this global problem.



Our main goal:

Our main goal with this foundation is repair broken families that deal with this global problem. According to World Drug Report in 2019, 35 million people worldwide have an addiction to drugs while 1 in 7 people recieve treatment. Our charity is willing to reach out to these people and give them the help they need to fight off their bad habits.



Care and Treatment

Our Foundation will put your money towards finding a solution to these bad habits. We will guide these drug users on the path of recovery through rehabitation centers and therapy. The Helping Hand Foundation wants to be that "helping hand" in the fight against addiction.
